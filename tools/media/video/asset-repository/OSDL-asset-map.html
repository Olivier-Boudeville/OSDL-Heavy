Overview

This page allows to keep track of the various assets and their transformations, so that they can be played back if needed.

Video Assets

Regarding animations coming from Reiner's Tilesets, see also these scripts:

  1. retrieve-reiner-content.sh, to download a local copy of the animations
  2. sort-reiner-mirror.sh, to sort animations by theme (see the result)
  3. process-reiner-content.sh, to trigger the full processing of all Reiner content
  4. process-reiner-individual-archive.sh, to process a particular archive from Reiner content (used by process-reiner-content.sh)

Animated objects

Their name is the human-readable identifier defined in animated-object-identifiers.txt. Same approach for outside looks (outside-looks-identifiers.txt), for attitudes (attitude-identifiers.txt) and directions (direction-identifiers.txt).

Instances in each category (object, outside look, etc.) are listed in the order defined in their repository (i.e. alphabetical order).

For each animated object, all available outside looks are listed and, for each outside look, all available attitudes are listed (in identifier order) between brackets. Unless specified otherwise, an attitude is available for all eight direction.

Characters

Humans and assimilated

Order is the one of animated-objects.id (alphabetical). Each asset has been transformed from the original BMP to PNG with options -strip -quality 100 -sharpen 1x.5 -trim (thus the scale is unchanged, unless specified otherwise with S=ratio%).

  • Anna : one outside look, Unarmed, with 3 attitudes: BeStill, Enjoy, Walk.

    Anna is a middle-aged female character suitable to represent average women in cities, or bartender, waitress, etc.

  • Arno : one outside look [S=85%], Unarmed, with 4 attitudes: BeStill, Run, Talk, Walk.

    Arno is a male character suitable to represent average men in cities.

  • Billy : one outside look, Unarmed, with 4 attitudes: Bend, BeStill, Talk, Walk.

    Billy can bend over, he can be a messenger, a singer, a poet or another kind of artist.

  • Bjorn: two outside looks [S=80%], Unarmed and ArmedWithASwordAndAShield, defined in one archive:
    • Unarmed , with 5 attitudes: BeStill, Look, Run, Talk, Walk.
    • ArmedWithASwordAndAShield , with 8 attitudes: Attack, BeenHit, BeStill, Look, Run, Thank, TipOver, Walk.
  • Burra [95%]: one outside look, ArmedWithASword, with 7 attitudes: Attack, BeenHit, BeStill, Run, TipOver, Talk, Walk.

    Burra is a prehistoric woman, armed with a sword (a machete), lipstick and red boots.

  • Elsa [90%]: one outside look, Unarmed, with 3 attitudes: BeStill, Thank, Walk.

    Elsa is a young blonde woman.

  • Freya: two outside looks [S=%] are available:
    • ArmedWithAnAxe
    • Unarmed
  • Stan

    Note that the animation for the Knit attitude is not specific to a given outside look, hence we put it in each look. All looks support at least following base attitudes: Attack, BeenHit, BeStill, Knit, Run, Talk, TipOver, Walk.

    There are 8 outside looks available for that character:

    • ArmedWithABow
    • ArmedWithAClub
    • ArmedWithAnAxe
    • ArmedWithAnAxeAndAShield
    • ArmedWithAStaff : base attitudes plus MagicAttack
    • ArmedWithASword
    • ArmedWithASwordAndAShield
    • Unarmed
