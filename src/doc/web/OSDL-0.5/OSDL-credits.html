Thanks for having helped us!

OSDL's credits in chronological order for the 0.5 version


The guys whom we would like to thank:


  • The brave GNU/Linux developers!
  • Sam Lantinga, for its famous SDL
  • Marc Petit, whose coloured makefiles and C++ split templates inspired us
  • Alexandre Makarenko, from who I learnt a lot
  • all the past or present members of our game creation club, CRI, including:
    • Arnaud Rivoira, for his flakes' dynamics, scenario ideas and his mathematical study of RPG-style set of rules, and for many other subjects as well
    • Thierry Mattray, for its scandinavian-style images, scenario ideas and all, and permanent cheerfulness
    • Sébastien Jouteau, for, among many subjects, his CPU-hungry fire
    • Murlock, for his hosted forum and his shared experiences
    • Tuan Tran, for his numerous contributions, including his great patience when LOANI was badly needing extensive testing
    • Asmodehn, for his interesting sharing of experience, regarding coding as well as remote travels
  • Stephen P. Booth, for his hints about fractal landscape generation and its beautiful xmountains program.
  • Various people we meet during club meetings and other events

I am totally aware that I forgot many people who contributed in some way, please let me address you a warm and collective thanks!
