The trailer of our first game

Overview

This section is about the trailer we are realizing in order to introduce our upcoming first game. It is merely a way for our club members to get used to work together, and to have a reachable target at first.

The trailer's story-board summarizes what is planned. Enjoy !

Rendering the falling of the snow

In order to model with great accuracy the flake's dynamics, one of our distinguished club member, Hirinkaël, wrote a study to justify the hypothesis we took to compute our squalls of snow with thousands of flakes falling their peculiar way. Thanks to them, we insist on the scandinavian theme of our trailer.
