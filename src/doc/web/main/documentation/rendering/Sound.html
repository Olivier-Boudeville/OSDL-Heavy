Quick overview

The purpose of this section is to give some hints about sound management. See also our Audio with SDL section.

Sound management

Recording

Under GNU/Linux, one may use krecord (the KDE sound recorder) which we found more useful than its Gnome counterpart. It produces wave files. wavrec does not seem to generate valid wave files.

Listening

Wave files

Lighter than xmms, wavplay or playwave (test program from SDL_mixer) can be used.

OggVorbis files

ogg123 does it for you.

Converting

From wave to OggVorbis

oggenc welcome-to-OSDL.wav produces welcome-to-OSDL.ogg.
